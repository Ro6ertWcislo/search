MICHAEL R. BLOOMBERG Both the military and Bloomberg embody a common spirit: the mission comes first. Teamwork. Communication. Adaptability. Integrity. These are just some of the skills and characteristics that transfer well from military service to a career at Bloomberg. When it comes to recruiting veterans, we’re upping our game. Software development, sales, data analysis, customer service and network support are all areas we are showcasing as places veterans should look to work at Bloomberg. Bloomberg provides training programs that allow veterans to transition more easily from military careers to the private sector.
